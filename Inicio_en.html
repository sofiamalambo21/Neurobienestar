What is Neurowellbeing?

Neurowellbeing is a project focused on the mental health of teenagers.It aims to understand and address the main risk factors that may affect their psychological wellbeing. It focuses especially on the most common mental disorders during this stage of life, such as depression, which today represents one of the most frequent and concerning issues among young people.




Symptoms and Causes of Depression

• Emotional symptoms (sadness, anxiety, lack of motivation, etc).

• Physical symptoms (tiredness, insomnia, changes in appetite, etc).

• Common causes in teenagers (school pressure, social media, bullying, family problems, hormonal changes, etc).
