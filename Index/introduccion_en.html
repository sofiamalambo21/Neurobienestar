We are two young women committed to the mental health of teenagers. Our purpose is to raise awareness about depression, break stigmas, and provide a safe support space where every young person can express themselves without fear. We believe in the importance of empathy, respect, and active listening; that why we work on projects that promote prevention and emotional well-being.
