Meet Vivi

Vivi is a kind and wise sloth who joins us in this project to remind us that it's okay to go slow and take care of our minds.
Vivi offer us:
Companionship: Always reminds us that we are not alone.
Advice Motivates us with positive phrases and self-care messages.
Calmness: With her tranquility, she teaches us that everything can be managed step by step.
Support: Encourages us to talk about our emotions without fear and to seek help when needed.
